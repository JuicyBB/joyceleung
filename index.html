Services

Basically, I make websites

Web Development

Developing pixel perfect design and code for personal or business websites. This includes hand crafted websites from top to bottom & websites built around a CMS.

Responsive Design

All websites are designed & coded with responsive in mind. Product will look & function just as well on desktop, tablet, mobile devices... or anything else in between.

Graphics & Print

In addition to web, graphic design services are also available. This includes web & printed graphic design. Ranging from logos & branding, to business cards & posters.

Maintenance

Some websites may be an ongoing project that will need maintenance or support. I also offer services for ongoing content updates & website maintenance.

Web Showcase

Some examples of web projects I have done.

Project Leadify

HTML/CSS Hybird App Ionic UX Design

CRM web application built with Ionic, node.js, angular and cordova. I took part as the core designer and UI developer for this project. This was built in a group of 5 in effort for a Hackathon contest within my employment at Move, Inc., which at the end recieved the People's Choice award in Jan 2015.

Lifestyle Score

iOS UX Design

This was an entry for the Hackathon 3.14 hosted by Move, Inc. in 2014. This UI design was based around the "Lifestyle score" idea to be implemented on realtor.com®. The UI design was completed in the 72 hour Hackathon, during my position as Interactive Web Developer at Move Canada. This submission received the Beautiful Design award.

MYST Vancouver

Wordpress Development Responsive UX Design

MYST Vancouver needed a website that explains the real life room escape game and ability to book online. The site's main purpose is to explain this new game to people that are unfamiliar with the concept. The game was first popularized in Asia, and now the MYST group would like to bring the craze into North America.

Envy Entertainment

Video BG Responsive UX Design UI Development

Envy Entertainment wanted website that gave a nightclub feeling, almost like putting someone in a party mood when they visit the website. They were looking for something edgy, young and flashy, almost making a viewer feel like they are at the party. A video background was chosen to make this effect come to life.
